Welcome to the drawing Page

I am a strong believer in open source, yet I also want to protect my own creations and the people I admire. I only share what I am willing and comfortable to share. All my work is source-available. I strive to be transparent about my work and do my best to avoid keeping trade secrets whenever possible. This approach aligns with how I learned from pioneers who generously shared their knowledge and experience with me. I release some of my work under various licenses, including MIT, CC BY 4.0, CC BY-NC, and GPL 3.0. Please respect my work and these licenses so that I can continue contributing to the community.
